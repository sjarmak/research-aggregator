#### [TeaRAG: A Token-Efficient Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation Framework](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025arXiv251105385Z/abstract)
**Source:** Academic Research | **Relevance:** 8/10  
**Why it matters:** TeaRAG is a new RAG framework that focuses on token efficiency, which is highly relevant to context engineering and information retrieval, especially in large-scale systems.  
TeaRAG introduces a framework that compresses retrieval content and reasoning steps to enhance token efficiency in RAG systems. This approach improves Exact Match scores while significantly reducing output tokens, making it a valuable contribution to efficient information retrieval.
